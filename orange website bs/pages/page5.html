CEOs Seminar
JORDAN 2022

MEA International Communication, Culture and Engagement

Keynote Speaker

Opening Keynote

Menny Barzilay

Menny is a Partner and Co-founder at Cytactic - a global cyber crisis management company. He is an internationally recognized cyber expert and was invited to speak at leading conferences and events in more than 30 countries. Additionally, Menny is the CTO of the Interdisciplinary Cyber Research Center at Tel-Aviv University. He is the founder of THINK: CYBER - a successful cyber security newsletter. Menny is a former CISO in the intelligence services of the Israeli Defense Force (Capt. Res.) and held several positions in the largest Israeli banking group.

She is the Editor in Chief of Africa Cybersecurity report, a continent wide annual report that highlights key trends and cybersecurity posture of different industries across African countries. She previously worked as a cyber risk consultant and Security Operation Centre Consultant. Brencil was recently elected into the ISACA Kenya Board as the Membership Director and is the recipient of the 2017 ISC2 ISLA EMEA, Upcoming Cybersecurity professional.
