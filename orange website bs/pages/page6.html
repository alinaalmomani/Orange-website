9:00 – Tue 17:00

Workshop: Artificial Intelligence (AI) Fundamentals

Workshop

AI is a growing technology with diverse use cases and the potential to radically change many industries as it continues to advance. The Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals course provides learners insight into the basics of AI functionality, application, risks and ethical considerations. The workshop offers learners the opportunity to expand on their understanding of AI concepts, principles, usage, associated risk and ethical requirements.

At the completion of this course learners will be able to:

  1. Explain the basic characteristics that define AI
  2. Explain what machine learning is and how it applies to AI
  3. Describe what robotic process automation is and how it differs from AI
  4. Explain statistical sampling populations, methods, statistical modeling, regression and how they apply to AI
  5. List the considerations for adopting AI
  6. List the different data governance roles and considerations
  7. Explain the specific impacts AI including potential risks and ethical considerations
